Teaching the way it should be done.

CoachBuddy was born from a simple frustration: too many students were getting answers when what they needed were better questions.

I have spent years watching students go through the motions — copying solutions, memorising syntax, passing exams — and leaving with no real understanding of what they learned. The traditional tutoring model, where the teacher solves the problem and the student watches, produces dependent learners. Not capable ones.

CoachBuddy is built on a different philosophy. We call it guided discovery. The platform — and the AI coach at its heart — never completes a task for a student. It asks the question that helps them complete it themselves. This is harder. It takes longer. And it works infinitely better.

The curriculum covers the skills that matter most in today's world: modern programming languages, web application development, and — for adults — financial markets. All taught with the same rigour, the same clarity, and the same refusal to take shortcuts.

Our Philosophy

What we believe about learning

🤔

Struggle is not failure

When a student wrestles with a problem, their brain is building permanent neural pathways. We protect that struggle. It is where the real learning happens.

🗣️

Plain language first

Every concept is explained in plain English before any code is introduced. Students understand the purpose before they learn the syntax. No jargon. No hand-waving.

🔁

Repetition builds mastery

We revisit concepts across multiple modules. By the time a student finishes a course, foundational ideas have been seen, applied, and tested enough times to be genuinely retained.

🎯

Real skills, not certificates

A CoachBuddy student who completes a course can build something with what they learned. That is the test. Not a score on a multiple-choice quiz.
