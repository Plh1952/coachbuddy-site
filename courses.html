School Subjects Tutoring

The core of CoachBuddy

This is where CoachBuddy began. An AI tutor that helps students understand their school curriculum — not by giving answers, but by asking the right questions. Maths, sciences, languages, history — the AI coach adapts to whatever your child is studying.
